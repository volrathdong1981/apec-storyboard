第4幕 APEC 理念 55词 1:20 - 1:50
But here's the thing — these birds can't make it alone. If even one country along the way doesn't protect its wetlands, the journey breaks.

That's why they need everyone to work together. And you know what? That's exactly what APEC is about. We're like one big family across the Pacific — helping each other, sharing with each other, and growing together.
但关键是——这些鸟儿没法独自完成旅程。如果沿途哪怕一个国家不保护湿地，这趟旅程就断了。

所以它们需要所有人一起合作。你知道吗？这恰恰就是APEC的意义。我们就像太平洋两岸的一个大家庭——互相帮助，彼此分享，共同成长。
